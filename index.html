About us

Welcome to CATfeine, where every cup of coffee comes with a side of whiskers and purrs. We are more than just a café — we are a cozy space where coffee lovers and cat lovers unite. Our mission is to create a warm, relaxing environment where people can enjoy high-quality drinks, delicious treats, and the calming presence of our friendly feline companions. All our cats are rescues, living their best lives while waiting to meet their forever families — maybe even you! Whether you're here for a latte, a quiet moment, or a furry cuddle, you're always welcome at CATfeine. Come for the coffee, stay for the cats.
